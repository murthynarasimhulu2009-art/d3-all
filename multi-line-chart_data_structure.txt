Explanation:
- date – temporal field that defines the horizontal position of each point on the time axis.
- price – quantitative field representing the price to be plotted vertically.
- symbol – nominal field that groups the data into separate lines (AAPL, GOOGL, MSFT) and drives the color scale.
- volume – quantitative field present in the data but not mapped in the current layer; could be used for additional encodings (e.g., tooltip or size). 

The data is a flat array; there are no nested arrays such as nodes or links.
