Explanation of fields:
- id – integer identifier, not visualized directly but useful for data joins.
- value – quantitative field representing the measurement to be plotted; also used as the x‑scale domain.
- category – nominal (categorical) field that determines the color of each circle via the “color” ordinal scale.
- x – numeric copy of *value* used for the x‑encoding; the dodge transform will adjust its position to avoid overlap.
- y – numeric vertical position; a transform adds a constant offset (100) before rendering, placing the swarm near the middle of the chart.

The data does not contain nested arrays (no nodes/links); it is a flat array of objects.
