Explanation of fields  
- group: categorical (nominal) field that groups rows into separate ridgeline “layers”. It acts as a series identifier.  
- x: quantitative field representing the horizontal coordinate for each bar (and ridge).  
- density: quantitative field representing the magnitude (height) of each bar; also used to scale the vertical offset of the ridgeline.  

The data array is flat; there are no nested arrays such as nodes or links. The chart reads this array directly as its data source.
