Explanation:
- task: identifier for each row, displayed on the y‑axis as a band.
- start / end: temporal bounds that define the horizontal span of each bar.
- progress: numeric progress value (0‑100) that could be shown as a tooltip or additional visual encoding.
- category: categorical label used to assign a colour from the “Set2” ordinal palette.

The data consists solely of this flat array; there are no nested arrays such as nodes or links.
