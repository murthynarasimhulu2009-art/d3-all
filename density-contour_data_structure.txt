Explanation of fields:
- x – numeric (quantitative) field representing the horizontal position of a point in the plot.
- y – numeric (quantitative) field representing the vertical position of a point.
- density – numeric (quantitative) field giving the density estimate for that (x, y) location; used for coloring the contour paths.

The data does not contain nested arrays (no nodes/links). It is a flat array of point objects that will be transformed by the “contour” transform to generate contour paths. The resulting contour objects will have a `path` string and a `density` value used for the `fill` encoding.
